About TCS

Company Overview

TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) is a leading global IT services, consulting, and business solutions provider. Founded in 1968, TCS has grown to become one of the largest and most respected IT companies in the world, headquartered in Mumbai, India.

With over 500,000 employees across 50+ countries, TCS serves clients in over 180 countries. The company is recognized for its commitment to innovation, sustainability, and delivering transformative digital experiences.

History

TCS was established in 1968 as a consulting firm by J.R.D. Tata, the founder of Tata Group. Initially focused on providing business consulting services, the company expanded into IT services in the 1970s. By the 1990s, TCS had become a major player in the global IT services market, leveraging its expertise in software development and IT infrastructure.

Throughout its history, TCS has been at the forefront of technological advancements, playing a key role in the digital transformation of industries worldwide.

Industry-Specific Solutions

Healthcare

TCS delivers end-to-end IT solutions for the healthcare sector, including electronic health records (EHR), telemedicine platforms, and AI-driven diagnostics. Our solutions enhance patient care, improve operational efficiency, and drive digital transformation in healthcare organizations.

Finance

In the finance industry, TCS provides robust IT solutions for fraud detection, risk management, and regulatory compliance. Our solutions help financial institutions enhance security, streamline processes, and improve customer experiences.

Retail

For the retail sector, TCS offers solutions that enable omnichannel retailing, supply chain optimization, and personalized customer experiences. Our technology-driven solutions help retailers enhance customer engagement and drive sales growth.
